# Impeccable

## Design Context

### Users

Adult amateur footballers on a Finnish team. They open session links from the
team chat on their phones — at home the night before training, in the car, or
standing at pitch-side in daylight. The job: quickly understand what tonight's
training is, what each drill looks like (video), and what the coach wants them
to focus on. Secondary user: the coach (Niko), who shares the links and
occasionally opens the public page to check how it reads. Admin UI is out of
scope for design work; it deliberately stays plain.

### Brand Personality

Playful, warm, sporty. The feeling of Sunday-league football among friends:
training is play, not work. Not childish and not corporate — grown-ups who
take fun seriously. Copy is in Finnish and already lightly playful (the 404
says "Se meni ohi maalin").

### Aesthetic Direction

Light theme only (pages are read outdoors in daylight; sun-readable contrast
matters). Amateur-football material world as the design vocabulary: grass
pitch, chalk lines, training cones, bibs, jersey numbers, tactics board.
Playfulness comes from color, shape, and detail — not goofy letterforms.
Anti-references: generic SaaS landing pages, dark "gamer" sports apps,
children's-app cuteness.

Constraint: body font is Manrope by the owner's explicit choice — do not
replace it. Display/heading typography may be added around it.

### Design Principles

1. **Pitch-side legibility first** — readable on a phone in sunlight; type
   contrast and size beat decoration every time.
2. **Football materials, not football clichés** — grass green, cone orange,
   chalk white as a palette; numbered drills like jersey numbers; no stock
   photos, no swoosh gradients.
3. **Playful in the details, calm in the structure** — layout stays simple
   and scannable; personality lives in headings, badges, numbers, empty
   states, and the 404.
4. **The session page is the product** — it's what players actually open;
   every design decision is judged there first, drill library second.
5. **Nothing slows the pitch-side glance** — no heavy media above the drill
   list, no animations that delay content.
